How to Play

Welcome to Neon Bounce! Control your paddle to keep the ball in play and break through all the bricks.

  • Move your paddle using the mouse or left/right arrow keys
  • Don't let the ball fall below your paddle
  • Break all the bricks to advance to the next level
  • Collect power-ups for special abilities
  • You have 3 lives to start - don't lose them all!

Ready to challenge yourself? Press the Play Now button and start bouncing!
